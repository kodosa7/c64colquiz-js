Instructions

  • There are 16 colours marked in hexadecimal codes from 0 to F.
  • Match the hex code that appears in the middle of the screen to the colour on the left or right section of the playfield by clicking on it before the timer runs out.
  • Right answer gets +1 point.
  • Wrong answer ends the current game.
  • The more points you get, the quicker is the timer.
  • When you reach 30+ points, the timer speed stops decreasing.

Good Luck!
